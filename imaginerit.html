II - 2022

Adobe Illustrator

Passion Project...

My freshman year at RIT, I entered independently. I went through several layouts and eventually came up with a striking visual of a tiger's eye, reflective of the iconic RIT tiger mascot. The dramatic colors and composition make for an overall captivating poster.

The sketch versus the final vector illustration.

III - 2023

Cinema 4D and After Effects (6 seconds)

...turned Class Project

While I had planned to independently enter the annual poster design competition again, my 3D class made it mandatory. I took inspiration from holograms and augmented-reality animations in movies, utilizing Cinema 4D's physical renderer in combination with luminated materials to create realisitic, glowing stills and a short animated movie.

The unedited render (left) versus final poster (right).
Cinema4D, 13x18" 150ppi, 6s (180 frames @ 30fps).
