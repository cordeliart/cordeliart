Imagine RIT Poster Compilation

Year — Spring 2022, 2023

Tools — Various Media

Dimensions — 13 × 18"

Synopsis — Imagine RIT is an annual festival held during spring at the Rochester Institute of Technology, celebrating technological advancements by demonstrating cool gadgets and gizmos to locals and students. These are my entries for the annual poster competition.

2022

Adobe Illustrator, 13 × 18" 300ppi

During my freshman year at RIT, I entered independently. I went through several layouts and eventually came up with a striking visual of a tiger's eye, reflective of the iconic RIT tiger mascot. The dramatic colors and composition make for an overall captivating poster.

↑

2023

Cinema4D & After Effects, 13x18" 150ppi, 6s (180 frames @ 30fps)

While I had planned to independently enter the annual poster design competition again, my 3D class made it mandatory. I took inspiration from holograms and augmented-reality animations in movies, utilizing Cinema 4D's physical renderer in combination with luminated materials to create realisitic, glowing stills and a short animated movie.
