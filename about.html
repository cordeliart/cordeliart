Hi there! My name is Lena Pang. I do math & design.

Wow! Those are so different!

Not really. They're just different forms of problem solving. Creating analytical solutions through math, and creating experiential solutions through design—they're one in the same. And I love them both.

Aside from problem solving, I enjoy traveling the world, playing piano, and listening to music (especially Radiohead and Rachmaninov). I also love learning languages: I've studied French, Japanese, and Russian both in school and independently. Thinking in the structure and syntax of another language is truly fascinating, and the ability to connect with other cultures is priceless.
