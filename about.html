Hello there!

My name is Lena Pang, though you might've seen my work under the pseudonym Cordeliart—which stems from my middle name, Cordelia. I am a designer and multimedia problem solver currently based in the United States.

Aside from design, I enjoy solving puzzles, traveling, and skiing. I love listening to Radiohead and Rachmaninov, and I've played piano for almost fifteen years now.
