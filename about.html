Hello there!

My name is Lena Pang, though you might’ve seen my work under the pseudonym Cordeliart—which stems from my middle name, Cordelia. I am a multimedia designer currently based in the United States.

When I’m not sketching or playing around with Illustrator, I enjoy watching YouTube, traveling, and skiing. I am an avid listener of Radiohead and Rachmaninov, and I've played piano for almost sixteen years.

I love learning new languages, including both world languages and coding languages. I am moderately conversational in French, albeit getting more rusty by the day because I transitioned into learning Japanese in early 2020. こんにちは！私の名前はリナ・パンです。どうぞよろしくお願いいたします！ I also spend a lot of my time playing around in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Lua and more. (In fact, this website is completely original!)

I am currently earning my BS in Applied Mathematics and BFA in New Media Design (and a minor in Japanese) at the Rochester Institute of Technology in New York. As I am in still in school, I am not looking for a full time job quite yet, but feel free to reach out with any questions—I'm always happy to chat!
