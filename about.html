Hi there! My name is Lena (Cordelia) Pang. I solve problems.

Specifically, math and design problems—two sides of the same creativity-stretching, mind-bending, detail-attentive coin. I combine my visual and analytic skills to make beautiful, effective, and efficient work.

Aside from problem-solving, I love listening to music (especially Rachmaninov and Radiohead), skiing, and learning languages. I've dipped my toes in French, Japanese, and Russian, and was fortunate enough to have the opportunity to study abroad for a summer in Kanazawa, Japan, during my time at university.

If you have any inquiries, send me a message at lena@cordeliart.com!
