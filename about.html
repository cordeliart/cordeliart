Hello there!

My name is Lena Pang, though you might’ve seen my work under the pseudonym Cordeliart—which stems from my middle name, Cordelia. I am a designer and multimedia problem solver currently based in the United States.

When I’m not sketching or playing around with Illustrator, I enjoy watching YouTube, traveling, and skiing. I love listening to Radiohead and Rachmaninov, and I've played piano for almost fifteen years now.

I am moderately conversational in French, albeit getting more rusty by the day because I switched to learning Japanese in early 2020. こんにちは！私の名前はリナ・パンです。どうぞよろしくお願いいたします！
