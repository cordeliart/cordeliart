Biome V1

23 June - 23 July 2020 | Adobe Illustrator, 2.5x3.5" x56

Over the course of the pandemic, I designed a deck of cards to reflect the beauty of nature--of the world we all dearly missed.

Each suit is a different biome with a unique color scheme.
Each card is a different species within that biome.
And each deck is 56 pieces of art tucked neatly into a box.

The Process

begins with research and quick study-sketches, aiming to capture the character and movement of each animal.

Then I do various pose digital pose sketches, working through several compositions to find the best one.

Finally, it comes time to render in Adobe Illustrator, using the colors and gradients I've determined for each suit.

Once I finished all 56 illustrations, I compiled everything (including the numerals and custom suits) into an AI document with the proper formatting for print.

The Campaign

After beginning to design the deck, word spread among friends and family about my upcoming creation, and demand gradually increased. So I decided to create a Kickstarter to see if I could fully realize my vision, and have Biome V1 printed in high quality, in large quantities.

The campaign ran from June 23rd to July 23rd, 2020, and successfully raised $6,844.

This allowed me to print over 1,000 decks through US Playing Card Company b6(the creator of the traditional Bicycle decks)--and distribute over 500 that were preordered during the campaign.
