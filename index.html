ABOUT

My name is Lena Pang, though you might’ve seen my work under the pseudonym Cordeliart—which stems from my middle name, Cordelia. I am a designer currently based in the United States.

When I’m not sketching or playing around with Illustrator, I enjoy watching YouTube, traveling, and skiing. I also love math and (romantic classical) music (especially Rachmaninov's second and third piano concertos). In fact, I've played piano for almost fifteen years now.

I am moderately conversational in French, albeit getting more rusty by the day because I switched to learning Japanese in early 2020. こんにちは！日本が大好きです。日本の文化と建築はきれいですから。

For any commissions, questions, or comments, please contact me at lenacordeliart@gmail.com.
