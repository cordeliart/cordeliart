Imagine RIT 2023

14 December 2022 | Cinema4D / After Effects, 2K (3840 x 2160 px)

For this assignment, we had to enter into the annual Imagine RIT poster contest (see my entry from the previous year here). This year, I opted to express the technological, innovative side of the festival. Inspired by the holograms you see in movies, I utilized Cinema 4D's physical renderer in combination with luminated materials to create realisitic stills and a short animated movie.
